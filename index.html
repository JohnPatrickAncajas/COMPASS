About us

COMPASS, the Computer's Student Association, is your compass in the world of technology. We're here to guide you through the latest developments, offer support, and connect you with like-minded individuals passionate about all things computing!

Vision

Our vision at COMPASS is to create a tech-savvy community that embraces innovation, collaboration, and inclusivity, contributing to a brighter technological future for everyone.

Mission

COMPASS is dedicated to providing individuals with the education and tools needed to excel in the ever-evolving tech world, fostering growth and proficiency in the field of computing.
